Campaign Updates

Bikeshare launches in Kigali

ITDP, UN Environment Program, and the Global Green Growth Institute partnered with bikeshare operator, Guraride, and the City of Kigali to launch a dockless bikeshare program in the Rwandan capital. Kigali has also been prioritizing non-motorized transport through other efforts, such as a car-free days. Read more

Advocacy to preserve cycle lanes in Jakarta

In June, police in Jakarta submitted a request to demolish protected cycle lanes on Jalan Sudirman and Jalan Thamrin despite strong use. ITDP Indonesia, with partners Bike2Work and C40, pushed the city to maintain the lanes. Read more about why bicycles are the future for Jakarta.

Ranking of cycling cities in Mexico released

The sixth version of the Ciclociudades Ranking evaluates infrastructure and policies that support cycle-friendliness at the local, regional, and national levels in Mexico. Check out our recent blog post on how Mexico City is moving to a new, better normal by making the temporary Av. Insurgentes cycle lane permanent.
